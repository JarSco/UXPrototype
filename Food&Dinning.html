Taniti Restaurants

Tanitit currently houses 10 restaurants throughout the isle. Experience the unique cuisine of the Tanitian people from 5 of the restaurants, these restaurants provide fish and rice dishes that are staples to the Tanitian diet with fresh local catches from the booming fishing industry. Feeling homesick? Taniti also has 3 restaurants that serve American-style meals and 2 restaurants that serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Looking to try cooking some Tanitian dishes yourself? Taniti has two supermarkets and two smaller grocery stores filled with fresh catches and locally grown produce along with any item you may need for your adventure. Taniti also has a convenience store that is open 24-7, so if you need a quick snack after a long venture the city of Taniti won’t leave you stranded.
