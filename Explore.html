Merriton Landing Entertainment

On the northern end of Yellow Leaf Bay, Merriton Landing is a rapidly growing entertainment district. If you're looking to experience the Tanitian nightlife, Merriton Landing houses many pubs, a microbrewery, a dance club, an arcade, and a bowling alley. For those who hope to see the sites of the island, Marriton Landing is a great launching point as it provides chartered fishing tours, helicopter rides, and a place to rent or buy snorkeling equipment. Marriton Landing is a great place to experience the culture of the Tanitian people as it has the national history museum, several art galleries and with most buildings using the native architecture.

Traverse Taniti’s Natural Beauty

Taniti is blessed with a truly vibrant natural landscape. With its pristine beaches, a lush tropical rainforest, and breathtaking views from the volcano, Taniti has a bit of everything for those with an adventurous spirit. To explore the island, Taniti houses many hiking trails that give great opportunities to see the island’s natural wonders, boat and bus tours for those who want to cover a lot of ground, and a zipline that cuts through the rainforest for those looking for a thrill.
