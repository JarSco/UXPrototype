Reach Taniti Through Air

Taniti City houses a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller airplanes. This airport is in the process of expanding and will be able to house larger jets by late 2026.

Reach Taniti by Sea

Once a week a cruise ship lands in Yellow Leaf Bay that allows passengers to board and exit the ship as needed. This cruise line launches from Hawaii US and visits many small islands throughout the pacific.

Transportation Options on the Island

Taniti island has several means of traversing the island. If you are looking to explore Taniti City and Merriton Landing the Tanitian government provides a public bus service that runs from 5 am to 11 pm. Taniti City also has several bike vendors that provide rental services. To explore further from Taniti City there is a car rental shop near the airport and several private bussing services that can take you to the more rural areas of the island.
