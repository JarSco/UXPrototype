Hotel Options

Taniti has many lodging options to provide for how you want to spend your time in Taniti. Whether it be a family owned hotel in the bustling Taniti city or a quaint bed and breakfast in the many small villages throughout the isle, you can rest easy while also getting first hand experience of Tanitian culture. All lodging is regulated by the Tanitian government to ensure all living spaces meet all health and safety requirements for its guests.

Relax at the Luxury Resort

Hoping to have the ultimate relaxing experience? Taniti holds a 4 star resort with direct access to the sandy beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay. This resort provides top of the line amenities, including a spa, a full indoor gym, a bar with live entertainment, and an all you can eat buffet that provides breakfast, lunch, and dinner services.
