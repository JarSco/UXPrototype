Looking for the next great vacation destination? Look no further as the great island of Taniti is quickly growing into one of the best tourist locations in the Pacific. Taniti is a tropical island with a surface area of 500 square miles, housing sandy and rocky beaches, a lush rainforest, and a central mountain range that holds a small active volcano. Taniti is home of the indigenous Tanitian people who are currently 20,000 strong, with a beautiful and vibrant culture.
